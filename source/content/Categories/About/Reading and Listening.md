### Podcasts
I listen to a lot of things. Too many things? These are all some of my favorites in regular rotation:

- Movement Memos by Kelly Hayes: Interviews and essential listens for creating effective, lasting movements
- It Could Happen Here by Cool Zone Media: up-to-date news (most of it terrible) and analysis (most of it pretty helpful) on what's happening
- Blowback: a podcast about American Empire - history we never learned in schools about American operations all over the world
- Cool People Who Did Cool Stuff by Margaret Killjoy: fun features of rad people throughout history, told in a windy and sometimes a little bit meandering way
- Code Switch by NPR: smart, funny commentary on serious and fascinating aspects of race politics and culture
- Latino USA by Futuro Media: incredible mix of features, culture reporting, and interviews all on latinidad in the us
- Tech Won't Save Us by Paris Marx: helpful commentary and info to both keep up with the latest tech-enabled fascism and to be better informed
- Gender Spiral with Alyy Beardsley and Babbet Thomas: really funny, witty interviews on all aspects of gender
- Better Offline by Cool Zone Media: another tech podcast with a solid political analysis
